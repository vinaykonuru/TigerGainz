What is TigerGainz?



TigerGainz is a workout buddy and group matchmaker for Princetonians that:

Analyzes your workout preferences

Finds Princetonians with the most FITTING workout preferences

Connects you with whoever you choose as your new workout buddy or group


TigerGainz does the hard work for you, removing the usual stress, uncertainty, and awkwardness of finding workout buddies and groups!

By finding the best workout buddies and groups, TigerGainz helps Princetonians stay active through better exercise experiences that are:

- more consistent because buddies and groups keep each other accountable

- of higher quality since studies show that working out with others can increase the duration and intensity by up to 200% (Kansas State University)

- more fun, whether you are on- or off-campus


How Does it Work?



Upon choosing and prioritizing your workout preferences, TigerGainz’s algorithm will find the most fitting workout partners or groups from our waiting list of participating Princetonians.

By default, TigerGainz will ensure your matches have the same exercise type preference; the rest of your preferences will be weighted according to the rank you assigned them in the form (Note: the weighted questions feature is still being developed).

Once matches have been made, you will see the profiles of your best potential workout buddies or groups. All of this can also be viewed in the “Matches” section. Then you can choose one of your matches. This will send an email to you and your partner confirming the match. Now, you can start working out together! If you do not like any of your matches you can click the “Put me on the Waitlist” button; please see below for more instructions.

If you do not get any matches, you will be automatically put on the waiting list. Then you can resubmit the form, search the directory, wait for someone to match with you, or remove yourself from the waiting list. To remove yourself from the waiting list, please go to the Waiting List section and click the “Take me off the waiting list please” button.

Be on the lookout for these emails from no.reply.tigergainz@gmail.com
